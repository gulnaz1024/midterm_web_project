Hijab is your crown


Reasons to wear hijab



1-Hijab is a fardh (required obligatory act, a Divine instruction by the Creator of the Heavens and the earth, Allah Almighty)

2-The most beloved thing through which you can seek Allah is to humbly and sincerely follow His instructions. If you love someone and this person refuses to listen to you or do the things that you love or ask him to do, will this be a loving relationship?

3-Hijab makes me feel strong and empowered. No one has access to my body. I’m in control.

4-You don’t blindly follow the society, fashion or what people think is pretty/fashionable. You’re a leader, not a follower.
